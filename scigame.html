SciGame 1: The Knowledge Quest

The Kingdom of Ignorance is a great problem, and only you can solve it. Before you, people have tried - but never come back. The Kingdom of Ignorance captures people and steals their brainpower. But you are different - you have loads of knowledge. Will you be able to beat the Kingdom of Ignorance?
Move with the arrow keys. Dark Grey blocks are solid ground. Light Grey blocks are trapdoors, that can be opened by pressing space. Purple Blocks will dissappear as soon as you find the Key. Guards will shoot you with questions, and you must answer them correctly to avoid getting captured.
Your mission is: Destroy all guards (shoot them by clicking), and find the Crystal of Wisdom!
